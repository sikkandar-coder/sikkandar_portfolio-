I am Sikkandar Sharpudeen, a Graphic Designer and Production Artist with extensive experience across branding, editorial, print, digital, and UI/UX design. Editorial · Magazine Design
FreshSlice Connection — Magazine Campaign
Objective

Redesign FreshSlice Pizza quarterly digest to move beyond transactional couponing and build an emotional connection by positioning the brand as part of life meaningful moments.

Concept

"Your Moments. Our Pizza." shifts focus from selling pizza to celebrating shared experiences. The cover adopts a lifestyle magazine aesthetic to elevate perceived value and encourage retention.

Design Approach
  • AI-Generated Lifestyle Imagery: Custom, warm, authentic visuals of people connecting over pizza — reinforcing the moments theme efficiently without a photoshoot.
  • Editorial Structure: Interior page guides and magazine-style layouts enhance readability and make the digest feel like a keepsake.
  • Strategic Brand Integration: Prominently featured the Proudly Canadian badge and bold 50% OFF typography — promotional clarity without compromising premium editorial tone.
Result

The redesign transformed the digest into a visually compelling, emotionally resonant piece. By blending AI-generated storytelling with clear promotional hierarchy, the cover strengthened brand identity, increased customer engagement, and elevated the publication from disposable flyer to desirable content.
